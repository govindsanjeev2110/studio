# **App Name**: AquaBloom Connect

## Core Features:

- Informational Content Hub: Provide detailed articles, guides, and resources about fish farming, horticulture techniques, and best practices, for reference by organization members.
- Product Marketplace: Create a platform for listing and selling fish and horticulture products, connecting farmers directly with buyers.
- Marketing & Sales Support: AI-powered marketing tool, generate tailored promotional content, suggesting effective marketing strategies and tools to maximize sales.
- Contact Us Form: Implement a user-friendly contact form for inquiries and support.
- About Us Page: Develop an informative About Us page detailing the organization's mission, values, and history.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Emerald green (#50C878) to evoke freshness and growth, connecting to horticulture and the aquatic environment.
- Background color: Light seafoam green (#E5F5EA), providing a calming and natural backdrop.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFD700), symbolizing prosperity and highlighting key elements such as calls to action.
- Font: 'Inter' for body text to ensure readability and 'Space Grotesk' for headlines, giving it a modern and tech feel.
- Use clear, illustrative icons representing fish farming and horticulture.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout to ensure organized and intuitive navigation.
- Incorporate subtle animations powered by Framer Motion for smooth transitions and engaging user interactions.
